How to play Chess?

There are 6 types of pieces in Chess

Bishops

Nights

Rooks

Pawns

Queen

King

Points For Each Peice

Pawn : 1 Point

Bishop : 3 Points

Night : 3 Points

Rook : 5 Points

Queen : 9 Points

King : No Points

Note : There are no points for king because he is the main peice in Chess.

Note 2 : There are two colours in Chess, they are White and Black.

How To Play

>

Bishop : Bishop can only move cross.

Night : Night can move in L shape, two steps front and 1 step right or left.

Rook : Rooks can move only in a straight direction.

Pawn : It can move at first either two steps or three steps front and it can go only straight but whlie killing
the other peices, it can move only one step cross.

Queen : It can move cross and straight.

King : King can move one step at any direction.
