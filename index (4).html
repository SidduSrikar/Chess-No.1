Night Fork

Night Fork occours very rarely. It is Nothing but Attaking Two Peices at a time.

Pinning

As you see above, if the Bishop move, it is a Check. So the Bishop cannot move. This is known as Pinning.

Double Attack

If you see the above pic, The Queen is attacking the Rook as well as the Night. So this is known as Double Attack.

Skewer Attack

Here the Bhishop is attacking the King as well as the Queen. If the King move, the bishop can capture the Queen immediately. This is known as Skewer Attack.

Discovered Attack

As you see here, if the Night moves the Queen is attacked by the Rook. This is known as Discovered Attack.

Discovered Check

If the Night move, it makes a Check with the Rook. This is known as Discovered Attack. The addvantage of Discovered Check is you can capture any peice you want by Checking the King. Here we got a Queen.

Note : There are Many more attacks in Chess. But these are the main Chess Tactics
